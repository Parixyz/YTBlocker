# Privacy Policy for YouTube Shorts Blocker

**Last Updated:** March 1, 2026

## Overview
The YouTube Shorts Blocker extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

## Data Collection
- **No Data Collection**: This extension does not collect any user data
- **No Tracking**: We do not track user behavior
- **No External Requests**: All quotes are stored locally and processed on your device

## Permissions Used
- `activeTab`: To detect when you're on YouTube
- `scripting`: To inject content on YouTube
- `storage`: To save your custom quotes locally (only on your device)
